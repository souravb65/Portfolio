Blockchain: Hypes and Future Ahead

A Little Introduction

This decade has seen multiple disruptive technologies to emerge one by one. Some them created a lot of interest in the industry and within research community. Technologies like Cloud Computing, Internet of Things (IoT), Machine Learning and Blockchain is one of them. Most of the researchers and top industry leaders believe that blockchain is the most disruptive technology of this decade. In simple terms blockchain is nothing but a secured chain of blocks. In technical terms blockchain is a decentralized, distributed peer-to-peer digital ledger.

The blockchain cannot be described just as a revolution. It is a tsunami-like phenomenon, slowly advancing and gradually enveloping everything along its way by the force of its progression.

When people listen the term blockchain, the first thing they think about is Bitcoin. Blockchain was first conceptualized by Satoshi Nakamoto in 2008. It gave birth to Bitcoin. For many years bitcoin outshined its parent. Slowly, researchers found many problems of bitcoin application and they started building their own cryptocurrency application using blockchain (Ex: Ethereum). Later, at the beginning of 2014 people started realizing that blockchain can be used in many other sectors not only on cryptocurrency.
